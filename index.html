Dialga Digits Guide

How to Play

Welcome to Dialga Digits! Your mission is to discover the mysterious number using cosmic hints and your mathematical intuition.

  • Select an Era: Choose from Ancient, Medieval, Renaissance, or Future eras with different number ranges
  • Make Guesses: Enter numbers and receive cosmic feedback
  • Use Powerups: Spend attempts to get hints, narrow ranges, or reveal digits
  • Watch the Weather: Cosmic weather changes based on your closeness to the number
  • Earn Achievements: Unlock special badges for accomplishments

Powerups Guide

Get Hint (2 attempts): Receive a cosmic insight about the number's properties
Narrow Range (3 attempts): Reduce the guessing range by 30%
Reveal Digit (5 attempts): See the first digit of the secret number

Number Personalities

Each number has a unique personality based on its mathematical properties:

  • Prime Numbers: Ancient and mysterious - divisible only by 1 and themselves
  • Perfect Squares: Balanced and geometric - form perfect squares
  • Fibonacci Numbers: Natural and growing - part of the golden sequence
  • Even Numbers: Symmetrical and cooperative - can be divided equally
  • Odd Numbers: Unique and independent - stand alone with character
